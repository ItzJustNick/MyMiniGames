# Pong - ItsJustNick
Pong is a 4 player game in which you hit a ball across the screen. This was one of my first C# projects at AIE.

---

## Objective
Hit ball into other players side.

---

## Player 1 Controls

**Left:**      Up Arrow

**Right:**     Down Arrow

## Player 2 Controls

**Left:**      N

**Right:**     M

## Player 3 Controls

**Left:**      C

**Right:**     V

## Player 4 Controls

**Left:**      W

**Right:**     S
